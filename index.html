About Our Campaign üåçüê∂

The Pet-Friendly City Campaign is dedicated to transforming urban environments into havens for pets and their owners. We believe that a truly modern city embraces all its inhabitants, including our beloved animals. Our initiatives focus on promoting responsible pet ownership, advocating for pet-friendly public spaces, and supporting local animal shelters. Together, we can build communities where pets thrive and enrich our lives.

Our Mission

To create inclusive urban spaces where pets are welcomed and cared for.

Our Vision

A future where every city is a truly pet-friendly haven for all.

Our Community

A growing network of passionate volunteers and pet lovers.
